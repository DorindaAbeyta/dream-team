Executive Summary

The website is designed to be a platform for people to anonymously post their dreams and share their experiences with others. The website has a high-level vision to provide a supportive and inclusive community where users can interact with each other in a positive manner. Users can up-vote and down-vote posts, and the most popular posts are displayed based on daily, weekly, and monthly popularity. The website also has a most recent feed and a set of pre-defined reactions, such as emojis and comments, to help users express their thoughts and emotions. To maintain a positive and safe environment, the website has a terms of use that prohibits bullying, explicit content, threats, and the sharing of personal information. The website also has an automatic moderation system that blocks certain words and an admin login for moderators to ban and delete posts if necessary. The website is optimized for both mobile and large screens and includes different categories, such as "Nightmares", "Funny", "Animals", "Lucid/OBE" and "School/Profession". Users can also save hashtags and search for posts within categories. Furthermore, users will have a personal journal that archives their old dream posts. Within these dream posts in the journal, users will be able to write comments on each post. These journal posts are private, but users can choose to share them with friends within the site if they wish. To ensure a manageable and readable experience, there will be an 8000-character limit on all dream posts and comments. The goal is to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly experience for users to share their dreams, connect with others, and keep track of their own dream journey in a personal and meaningful way.
