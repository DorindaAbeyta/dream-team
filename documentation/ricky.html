Persona

Name: Ricky Valenzuela

Age: 17 years old

Role: High School Senior

Technology: His parents bought Ricky a MacBook Air 13.3" Laptop for his birthday.

Attitudes & Behaviors: Ricky has a strong fascination with dreams and is eager to explore their meanings and suggestions. Because he is reflective, he consistently writes down his dreams the next day. Ricky is willing to share his dreams with his friends, demonstrating a desire to connect with others and be open about his experiences. Despite not connecting with his friends on the topic of dreams, Ricky continues to seek out others who share similar experiences.

Frustrations & Needs: Ricky struggles to find people who share his interest in dreams, which leaves him feeling isolated and unsupported. He is experiencing out-of-body experiences and is unsure whether they are dreams or something else, leading to confusion and a desire for clarity. Ricky wishes to find a group or community that shares his interest and experiences, so he can better understand them.

Goals: Ricky wants to connect with people who share his fascination with dreams and can relate to his experiences. He seeks to uncover the meanings behind his dreams, hoping to gain insight into his experiences and how they might relate to his life. Ricky aims to broaden his knowledge about dreams and out-of-body experiences, potentially searching into scientific or spiritual explanations. Ricky then intends to document his dreams, out-of-body experiences, and insights gained from his interactions with others in a journal, creating a record of his journeys.


User Story, Use Case, & Interaction Flow

User Story: As a power user I'm seeking to gain insight into my dreams by engaging with others who have had similar experiences, allowing me to document, reflect, and achieve greater clarity.

Interaction Flow

Pre-condition: Ricky has internet access and a device to explore the website.

  • Ricky discovers a dream community platform online that connects individuals with similar interests and experiences.

  • Ricky creates an account and sets up his profile, detailing his fascination with dreams and his recent experiences.

  • The home page displays a category lucid dreams/out-of-body.

  • Ricky clicks on the icon and enters the page where he views many posts that discuss this experience.

  • Many posts explain the difference between the two, and inform that people confuse out-of-body experiences with lucid dreaming.

  • Ricky engages with the community by posting about his own dreams and experiences, asking for interpretations and sharing thoughts.

  • Other users respond to Ricky's posts, and give their insights and interpretations.

  • Ricky discovers his profile page, where he notices that he has access to a journal section, allowing him to comment on and save all his dream experiences.

Post-condition: Ricky feels connected and supported. He has a better understanding of his dreams and lucid dreaming, and has documented everything in his profile's journal page.
