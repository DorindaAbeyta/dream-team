Persona

Name: Ricky Valenzuela

Age: 17 years old

Role: High School Senior

Technology: His parents bought Ricky a MacBook Air 13.3" Laptop for his birthday.

Attitudes & Behaviors: Ricky has a strong fascination with dreams and is eager to explore their meanings and suggestions. Because he is reflective, he consistently writes down his dreams the next day. Ricky is willing to share his dreams with his friends, demonstrating a desire to connect with others and be open about his experiences. Despite not connecting with his friends on the topic of dreams, Ricky continues to seek out others who share similar experiences.

Frustrations & Needs: Ricky struggles to find people who share his interest in dreams, which leaves him feeling isolated and unsupported. He is experiencing out-of-body experiences and is unsure whether they are dreams or something else, leading to confusion and a desire for clarity. Ricky wishes to find a group or community that shares his interest and experiences, so he can better understand his dreams and out-of-body experiences.

Goals: Ricky's wants to connect with people who share his fascination with dreams and can relate to his experiences. He seeks to uncover the meanings behind his dreams, hoping to gain insight into his experiences and how they might relate to his life. Ricky aims to broaden his knowledge about dreams and out-of-body experiences, potentially searching into scientific or spiritual explanations. Ricky then intends to document his dreams, out-of-body experiences, and insights gained from his interactions with others in a journal, creating a record of his journey.


User Story, Use Case, & Interaction Flow

User Story: I am looking to connect with like-minded individuals who share my experiences and interest in dreams and out-of-body experiences. Ultimately, this will help me document all my experiences and insights gained from interactions with others in a journal.
